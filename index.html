About Me

Hello! I am Mxolisi Nhlengethwa, I hold a diploma in music with over 10 years of experience as a pianist, playing both piano and keyboards. A highlight to my career was my 3-year tenure as the residential pianist at the renowned Royal Swazi Sun International Hotel. Iam currently expanding my musical horizons by working on multiple new productions. I am also a song writer and arranger. You can check some of my performances on the gallery below.
